Tags: email, newsletter, mailchimp, email list

Add registering users to your MailChimp list.

== Description ==
MailChimp extension for WordPress automatically subscribe users to a specific list upon creating an account.
Mass email subscription within the settings page also available.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
See the extension page for more info http://profilepress.net/downloads/mailchimp/

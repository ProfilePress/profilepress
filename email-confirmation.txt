Allow new users to confirm their email address before their account is activated or active.

== Description ==
ProfilePress email confimration add-on for WordPress ensure registered users verify or confirm their email address before their account is activated.
They won't be able to log in to their account if their email address is not confirmed.

See the extension page for more info http://profilepress.net/downloads/email-confirmation/
